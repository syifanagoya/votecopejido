'Black Mafia Family' Episode 2: Lamar is killing people, shocked fans say he's 'loco'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

'Black Mafia Family' has returned with its second episode of the season and we have mixed feelings about it. The episode saw things start to go wrong for the Flenory brothers with Terry Flenory (Da'Vinchi) being shot in the head at a parking lot.

The Starz show which follows Demetrius 'Meech' Flenory (Demetrius Flenory Jr) and Terry, two brothers from Detroit who rise from poverty to become cocaine kings picked up its pace this week. The season premiere had just shown us a glimpse of the beginning and introduced characters to audiences with the second episode 'Rumors' seeing the story pick up.

This week's episode started on a tense note as we saw Meech rush to the hospital after Terry is shot in the parking lot. The Flenory family is in a proper state with the mother Lucille (Michole Briana White) blaming Meech who doesn't accept it may have been his fault. We see Terry struggle with surgical complications after he was shot in the head and may lose his vision. Things aren't going great on the business front with the crib being compromised forcing the 50 gang to look for a new place to move the stash to.

Meanwhile, Lamar (Eric Kofi Abrefa) has been making moves on both the family front and trying to make a comeback in business as we see him surprise his adoptive daughter at school, a fact she is shocked by and distances herself from him. He also has been trying to get back in the game and looks to join up with the 12th Street boys. Meech's father is worried about the rest of the family, saying this time Meech has got to go for real or else the rest are going to end up hurt.

Demetrius meanwhile is on the hunt for the man who attacked Terry and beats up suspects.  At the hospital, although the one injured, Terry can only ask about his brother's welfare, with him wanting to still be in the game. But today's, episode belonged to Lamar as we see him go about town attacking people mercilessly and even killing giving off complete mental patient vibes with his shocking behavior.
