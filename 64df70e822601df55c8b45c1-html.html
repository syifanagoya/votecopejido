MLB News: Bowman Field: Important details about where the 2023 Little League Classic is being played

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, April 26, 2024

The 20-team Little League World Series is set to make a comeback. Following its cancellation in 2020, it was transformed into a competition exclusive to the United States in 2021.

Last year, despite the expansion of the tournament to encompass 20 teams, none could rival the remarkable performance of Hawaii.

They demonstrated exceptional supremacy on their path to clinching the championship title, emerging victorious against Curacao and claiming the coveted crown of the Little League World Series.

The scheduled dates for the Little League World Series 2023 are from August 16 to August 27. During the course of this 12-day tournament, in addition to the championship game on August 27, two other noteworthy events deserve attention.

One will be on Sunday, August 20, when the Philadelphia Phillies and the Washington Nationals participate in the MLB Little League Classic 2023 at Historic Bowman Field in Williamsport.

How old is Bowman Field in Williamsport?

Inaugurated in 1926, Bowman Field stands as the second-oldest stadium within the realm of minor league baseball.

Furthermore, Bowman Field served as the primary home venue for the Pennsylvania College of Technology's Wildcats for over 10 years, up until the conclusion of the 2021 baseball season.

How big is the Williamsport baseball field?

The dimensions of the playing field are two-thirds of the area of a standard professional baseball field.

This includes basepaths measuring 60 feet (18.3 meters), a 46-foot (14 meters) mound and, following adjustments in 2006, outfield fences situated at a distance of 225 feet (68.6 meters), shaping a quarter of a complete circular layout.

Who plays at Bowman Field in Williamsport, PA?

The stadium is under the ownership of the City of Williamsport, and Bowman Field serves as the home of the Single A Williamsport Crosscutters, as well as Penn College's baseball team. In a few days, it'll also host one of the most important baseball events of the year.
