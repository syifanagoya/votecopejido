Maria B Wiki (Designer) Age, Husband, Biography, Family &amp; More

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, January 26, 2024

Maria B (born 18 September 1983) is a Pakistani fashion designer, and entrepreneur from Karachi, Pakistan. She is well-known in all over the world for her best stylish and designer clothes.

After completing her fashion designing, she started his own online clothing store named “Maria B”. She sells the outfits designed by her in the store. Moreover, she worked with so many popular Pakistani models for the promotion of her brand.

On 23 March 2020, police took Maria’s husband into custody. According to sources, her husband “Malik Tahir Saeed” sent his servant (who is a patient of Corona) to the village without telling the police. As the whole world knows that Corona is an epidemic that grows by being in contact with others.

Despite knowing this, Tahir Saeed allowed his servant to go to the village. After receiving the news, the police went to his house at 12:30 am on 23 March and arrested him. The next day, Maria posted a video on social media in which she appealed to Pakistan Prime Minister Imran Khan to free her husband.
